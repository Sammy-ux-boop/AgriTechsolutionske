About AgriMap Solutions

AgriMap Solutions is a geospatial agriculture company helping farmers make data-driven decisions. Using GIS analysis, satellite imagery, and spatial data, we help farms improve productivity, identify water resources, and design efficient farm layouts. Our mission is to transform agriculture through smart mapping and precision farming technologies.

Our Services

Farm Mapping

Boundary mapping, land use planning, and spatial farm zoning using GIS technology.

Soil Suitability Analysis

Identify the most suitable crops based on soil properties, slope, and terrain analysis.

Borehole Site Identification

Use GIS and hydrogeological data to locate optimal groundwater drilling points.

Satellite Farm Monitoring

Monitor crop health and drought stress using satellite imagery.

Precision Fertilization

Optimize fertilizer application using spatial data.

Irrigation Planning

Design efficient irrigation systems using terrain and water source analysis.

Crop Health Monitoring

Identify crop stress and pest outbreaks using drones.

Land Use Planning

Plan farm layout for maximum productivity and sustainability.
